A P J Abdul Kalam


Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering.Read more...
Born: 15 October 1931, Rameswaram
Died: 27 July 2015, Shillong
Awards: Bharat Ratna, Veer Savarkar Award, Hoover Medal
Full name: Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam
Education: Madras Institute of Technology, Anna University
